Coauthor on Discovery of New Exoplanet

While researching for Dr. William Welsh in the exoplanet group at San Diego State University, Caleb wrote code to classify, visualize, and process data for the same team that discovered or contributed to the discovery of all 14 currently known circumbinary planets. His work culminated in coauthoring a paper on the discovery of a new exoplanet: TIC 172900988: A Transiting Circumbinary Planet Detected in One Sector of TESS Data Caleb created three of the figures featured in the paper that was published in the American Astronomical Society Journals.
